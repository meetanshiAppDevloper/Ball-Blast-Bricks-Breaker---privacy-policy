Privacy Policy – Bricks Bash Blitz Breaker

Effective Date: October 3, 2025

Overview

MEETANSHI TECHNOLOGIES LLP operates the Bricks Bash Blitz Breaker game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

Data Collection

  • No personal data is collected (including name, email, phone number, or location).
  • No analytics or usage statistics are collected.
  • No tracking across apps or websites is performed.

Permissions

The app does not request any sensitive device permissions (such as camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or storage).

  • The only permission the app may request is internet access if online features are enabled.
  • If you prefer an entirely offline experience, simply disable network access for the app on your device.

Gameplay Data

Any game progress (levels completed, scores, settings) is stored locally on the user's device and is not uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Third-Party Services & Advertising

The app does not integrate third-party advertising networks or analytics SDKs.

If such services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated and users will be informed.

Security

Because we do not collect or store personal data, there is minimal risk regarding personal data leakage.

All data remains under the user's control on their device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes to the app or legal requirements.

The Effective Date at the top will be updated when changes are made.
